# Overview of folders/files in this section 

1) "Ablation" folder corresponds to the section "**Ablation of metabolic feedback to genes highlights the role of feedback in regulating growth**" mentioned in the manuscript. The necessary codes/data to reproduce the results from this section are given
2) "Application_Other_Media" folder corresponds to the section "**Applying CausalFlux to predict growth/no-growth phenotype under different media conditions**" in the manuscript. The necessary codes/data to reproduce the results from this section are given
3) "CausalFlux_Comparison" folder corresponds to the section "**Real-world evaluation: Using CausalFlux to predict growth/no-growth phenotype in *E. coli***" mentioned in the manuscript. The necessary codes/data to reproduce the results from this section are given
4) "CausalFlux_Runs" folder provides all the code/data to perform 798 single-gene KOs
5) "GRN_Reconstruction" folder provides the code/information to obtain the *E. coli* GRN to perform gene KOs
6) "PROM_rFBA_CausalFlux" folder corresponds to the section "**Benchmarking CausalFlux with rFBA and PROM datasets**" mentioned in the manuscript. The necessary codes/data to reproduce the results from this section are given.
